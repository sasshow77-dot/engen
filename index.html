What is EnGen?

Your personal English learning platform

EnGen is an AI-powered platform that personalizes English instruction to your level, goals, and schedule. Over 170 workforce-aligned courses — built for real life at work and beyond.

📱
Learn anywhere, anytime
Access lessons from your phone, tablet, or computer — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Study at home, on the bus, or on your break.
🎯
Personalized to you
EnGen adapts to your English level and goals. Whether you want to communicate at work, with customers, or in daily life — the platform builds a path just for you.
👩‍🏫
Live classes + a coach
Join 8 live group classes every month to practice speaking. A personal EnGen coach is also there to help you stay on track and reach your goals.
🏆
Earn a certificate
Complete the program and receive an official certificate of completion with a certified English proficiency level — something you can show employers.
